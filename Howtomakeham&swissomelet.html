HOW TO MAKE : HAM AND SWISS OMELET

Ingridents

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of water
  • 1/8teaspoon salt
  • 1/8teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup cubed fully cooked ham
  • 1/4 cup shredded swiss cheese

Steps needs to follow

  1. In a small nonstick skillet,melt butter over medium-high heat.
  2. whisk the eggs,water,salt and pepper.
  3. Add egg mixture to skillet( mixture should set immediately at edges
  4. as eggs set, push to cooked edges towards the center, letting uncooked portion flow underneath.
  5. when eggs are set, place ham on one side and sprinkle with cheese; fold other side over filling. slide omelet onto plate.
